Roselia is an all-girl band in the BanG Dream! franchise. The group consists of five members, namely Minato Yukina on vocals, Hikawa Sayo on guitar, Udagawa Ako on drums, Shirokane Rinko on keyboard, and Imai Lisa on bass. They debuted with the song BLACK SHOUT. They're a popular gothic J-Rock band that is recognized at a near-professional level and is catching the eyes of the music industry and scouters.
